Design rationale:
  • Placement: New Cash Forecast tab on Property Financials (dashed outline in the mockup tab bar above). Visible on Active + Planning years. Not useful for historical years.
  • KPI strip: Property cash today, 12-mo projected, lowest point (with cause), average levered cash flow. Color-coded by whether the low point crosses the min-reserve threshold.
  • Combo chart: Green inflow bars above zero, red outflow bars below, cyan line overlay = running cash balance. Safety-threshold line makes the danger obvious. Low-point bubble calls out the worst month.
  • Breakdown table: Shows the property pro-forma logic — operating inflows, operating outflows, levered cash flow, then capital events (TI, capex, distributions, reserve draws). You can point to any cell and explain where the number came from.
  • Assumptions panel: Right side. Shows what's auto-linked from other systems (✓ linked badge) vs. what needs manual input. Tweak an assumption, hit Recompute, chart updates.

Data sources it'll need: rent_roll (for contractual rent + escalations), budget_line_items (for opex), exec_liabilities (for debt service schedule), a new table or fields for planned TI/LC/capex events + partner distributions + property-level starting cash.
